Barack Hussein Obama II is an American politician who served as the 44th President of the United States from 2009 to 2017.
He is the first African American to have served as president, as well as the first born outside the contiguous United States.
He previously served in the U.S. Senate representing Illinois from 2005 to 2008, and in the Illinois State Senate from 1997 to 2004.
Obama was born in Honolulu, Hawaii, two years after the territory was admitted to the Union as the 50th state.
